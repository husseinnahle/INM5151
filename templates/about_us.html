About EZCoding





EZCoding is a new and more interactive way to learn
how to code with the inclusion of a tree of progression that allows you to keep track of
your learning curve and to feel as compelled by learning as if you were progressing in a video game!







The mission of this website is to allow a large variety of people, from
young children in middle school to adults who want to learn programming in
a more stimulating way!
